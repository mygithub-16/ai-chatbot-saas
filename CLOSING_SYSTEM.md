# Closing System

The chatbot should gently move qualified visitors toward action:

- Answer the direct question first.
- Identify intent: booking, reschedule, cancellation, service inquiry, or general chat.
- Collect one missing slot at a time.
- Keep a short memory of the workflow state per session.
- When the visitor shows buying intent, encourage booking, quote requests, calls, or lead submission.

Lead quality can be scored from urgency words, buying words, and whether contact information was provided.

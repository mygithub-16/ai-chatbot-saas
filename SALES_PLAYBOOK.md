# Sales Playbook

## Positioning

ECHURA gives small businesses a 24/7 AI receptionist that answers customer questions, captures leads, and summarizes funnel performance.

## Discovery Questions

- How many customer messages do you miss each week?
- Do customers usually ask about booking, pricing, product availability, or support?
- Where do your leads currently come from?
- Do you need appointment scheduling, quote requests, or ecommerce support first?

## Demo Flow

- Configure a business profile.
- Send a booking or pricing question.
- Show workflow memory and analytics.
- Close on missed-lead recovery and faster response time.
